### 2. Dark Fantasy Terminal

ダークファンタジー×ターミナル。少しダークな雰囲気。

```
A dramatic 3D digital art banner featuring "cc ensemble" in an
elaborate gothic-tech font with luxurious golden neon accents. The
text emerges from a swirling vortex of terminal panes and code
streams. Rich gradients flow from molten gold through crimson red,
deep sapphire blue, magenta pink, emerald green, and royal purple.
Behind the lettering, ethereal command chains curve gracefully like
ancient scrolls, with tmux borders glowing like ornate filigree.

highlights against deep shadows. Set against a pristine white
background with subtle circuit patterns. Cinematic poster meets dark
fantasy coding environment. --ar 16:5
```
